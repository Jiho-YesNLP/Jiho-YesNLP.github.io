I am an Assistant Professor in the [Department of Computer Science](https://campus.kennesaw.edu/colleges-departments/ccse/academics/computer-science/index.php) at [Kennesaw State University](https://www.kennesaw.edu/), where I direct the **[Cognition and Learning Design Lab](https://yesnlp.github.io)**. My research focuses on the applied science of **Natural Language Processing (NLP)** and **Information Retrieval (IR)** across a variety of domains, including healthcare, education, and project management.

**Research Interests:** Knowledge Representations &middot; Human-Computer Interaction &middot; AI/ML in Educational Settings &middot; Educational Technology Design and Evaluation &middot; Educational Data Mining &middot; Learning Analytics &middot; Student and User Modeling

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the [University of Kentucky](https://www.uky.edu/), where I worked on neural representations for biomedical information retrieval under the supervision of [Dr. Ramakanth Kavuluru](https://cs.uky.edu/~rak/). Before joining KSU, my work spanned biomedical NLP, precision medicine retrieval, and entity normalization.

I am always looking for motivated students interested in NLP, AI in Education, and Information Retrieval. Feel free to reach out if you are interested in joining the lab.
