This study introduces the **YouTube Learners' Questions on Bloom's Taxonomy Dataset (YouLeQD)**, which compiles questions posed by learners in YouTube lecture video comments.

The research develops two RoBERTa-based classification models that utilize Large Language Models to identify questions and assess their cognitive complexity according to Bloom's Taxonomy. By analyzing the cognitive complexity of learner-posed questions and their interaction metrics, the study provides valuable insights for developing AI models for education.

**Team:** Nong Ming, Sachin Sharma, Dr. Jiho Noh

**Funding:** Interdisciplinary Seed Grant, Kennesaw State University

**Code:** [github.com/YesNLP/QYTL](https://github.com/YesNLP/QYTL)

**Paper:** [IEEE ICSC 2025](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/11036279)
