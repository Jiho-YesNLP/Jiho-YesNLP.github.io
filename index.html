Kennesaw State University

Marietta, GA

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Kennesaw State University, where I direct the Cognition and Learning Design Lab. My research focuses on the applied science of Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Information Retrieval (IR) across a variety of domains, including healthcare, education, and project management.

Research Interests: Knowledge Representations · Human-Computer Interaction · AI/ML in Educational Settings · Educational Technology Design and Evaluation · Educational Data Mining · Learning Analytics · Student and User Modeling

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Kentucky, where I worked on neural representations for biomedical information retrieval under the supervision of Dr. Ramakanth Kavuluru. Before joining KSU, my work spanned biomedical NLP, precision medicine retrieval, and entity normalization.

I am always looking for motivated students interested in NLP, AI in Education, and Information Retrieval. Feel free to reach out if you are interested in joining the lab.
