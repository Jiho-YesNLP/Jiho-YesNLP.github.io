Jiho Noh is an assistant professor of computer science at Kennesaw State University. His primary research interests are on the fields of natural language processing with applications to intelligent search.

Topic keywords include:
  • Neural representation learning for knowledge discovery
  • Conversation techniques in information retrieval
  • Text processing including data mining, information extraction, entity recognition and knowledge database construction
  • Natural language generation

If you are interested in discussing research topics, please contact me via email.
